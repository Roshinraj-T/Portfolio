ABOUT

Hi there! I’m Brenna — a creative director and brand strategist currently in Northern California. For the last 15+ years, I've produced hundreds of B2B and B2C projects for corporate, CPG, retail, government, and nonprofit clients in agency, freelance, and in-house positions on time and under budget by up to 10%.

I love crafting branded multi-channel campaigns that leverage user/consumer research and analytics to get results. Deep insights into target audiences, what motivates them, and where to best reach them eliminates guesswork on campaign development, resulting in bulletproof creative using the right words and the right images and presented in the right places. Initially trained as an art director and copywriter at the Academy of Art University, my skillset has evolved to include digital and search ads, SEO, email marketing, social media, UX/UI, events, PR, and media relations. I can execute any of these projects from start to finish — either as a self-directed soloist or as a collaborative member of a team. Whatever it takes to get the job done!
